## Key Points from NEPQA 2025 - PV Inverter

- Match Nepal grid voltage/frequency (50Hz ±2.5%)
- MPPT efficiency ≥95%, inverter efficiency ≥95% (≤5kVA) or ≥97% (>5kVA)
- IEC test certificates required (61727, 62116, 62891, 62109-1/2)
- Certification from CBTL/RETL/NCB/RECB (IECEE or IECRE listed)
- Importer agreement with manufacturer (warranty ≥5 years)
- Voltage 400±10% VAC (3-phase) or 230±10% VAC (single)
- THD <5%, Power factor >0.99
- IP65 protection, DC reverse polarity protection, anti-islanding
- Built-in meter and data logger
- Label: manufacturer, model, rated power, voltage, MPPT range, serial number
